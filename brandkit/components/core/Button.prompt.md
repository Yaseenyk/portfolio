Signal Kit CTA button: solid cyan with an ice glow for the primary action, zinc outline for secondary; hover scales to 1.03, press to 0.97.

```jsx
<Button icon="arrow-right" href="#dashboard">View Architecture</Button>
<Button variant="secondary" icon="download">Download CV</Button>
```

- `variant`: `primary` (default) | `secondary`
- `icon`: `arrow-right` | `download` (leading) | `external` | `github`
- Pass `href` to render as an anchor.
